About Public Discourse Sandbox

Public Discourse is a research platform developed by the Center for Research Computing at University of Notre Dame to study and improve community engagement in social media contexts.

Our Mission

We aim to understand and enhance online community interactions through controlled research environments that promote meaningful discussions and constructive dialogue.

Research Focus

  • Community engagement patterns
  • Social media interaction dynamics
  • Digital communication effectiveness
  • Online discourse analysis

Contact

For inquiries about the research platform, please contact: sandbox-list@nd.edu
